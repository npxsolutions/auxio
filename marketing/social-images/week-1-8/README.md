## Design constraints

- **Canvas** 1200×627, optimised for LinkedIn and X link previews.
- **Palette** cream `#f3f0ea` · ink `#0b0f1a` · cobalt `#1d5fdb` · emerald `#0e7c5a` · oxblood `#7d2a1a`.
- **Typography** `"Instrument Serif", Georgia, serif` for display, `"Geist Mono", ui-monospace, monospace` for labels. Falls back gracefully when fonts are not available.
- **No emoji, no stock photography, no full-bleed gradients.** Subtle panel fades only.
- **Rings, not fills** for schematic nodes. Corners 2px on chips, 10px on panels. Strokes 1.5px.
- Each SVG is self-contained and accessible via a `<title>` at the top.
