## Long description (~1,200 chars)
Meridia is the operating layer for merchants selling across more than one channel. We unify inventory, orders, listings, repricing, and true profit into a single workflow — so operators stop alt-tabbing between marketplace dashboards, exporting CSVs into Excel, and reconciling fees by hand at month-end.

Sellers using Meridia typically connect three to twelve sales channels and consolidate them into a single source of truth. Inventory updates propagate in under a minute. Orders land in one feed. Listings push to multiple channels at once with channel-specific copy generated by an AI listing agent. Profit is computed after fees, shipping, returns, and per-region VAT or sales tax — so what you see is what you keep.

Meridia is built for the operator who runs the back office, not the merchandiser who lives in one channel&rsquo;s dashboard.
