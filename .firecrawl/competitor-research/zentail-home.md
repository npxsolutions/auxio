## Know where you’re losing sales – and fix it.

Zentail gives you the clarity to identify gaps in your sales on every marketplace, whether from new requirements, inventory issues, or listing inaccuracies. You’ll know exactly what’s wrong and how to fix it, fast.

By centralizing all your sales data, Zentail provides a real-time view of your multichannel business. With automated insights and actionable recommendations, you can solve issues quickly. Make changes directly across any channel – no support tickets, delays, or back-and-forth emails required – keeping you in control and driving your team forward with smarter, data-backed decisions.

## Say goodbye to templates and recipes.

No configurations, no mappings – just fast, effortless listing automation. Zentail’s SMART Types technology revolutionizes how you list products, making it possible to list a single SKU or an entire catalog with just one click.

As marketplaces evolve and introduce new requirements, SMART Types automatically adjusts how your data is sent to ensure your listings remain compliant and optimized. This means less time spent maintaining listings, mappings and configuration and more time focused on growing your business.
