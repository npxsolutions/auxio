Frequently asked questions

Are all Veeqo features free to use?

**Shipping features are free for everyone**. Carriers pay us a small commission on each label you buy, so we can keep our shipping software free for every seller.

Power features, including inventory sync and digital picking, are available at additional cost. [See our pricing page](https://www.veeqo.com/pricing) for more information.

Are Veeqo’s discounted shipping rates competitive?

Yes, you’ll get instant access to the **lowest commercially available rates**, based on our pre-negotiated pricing from UPS, USPS, FedEx, and DHL and up to 5% back with Veeqo Credits.

You can also connect your carrier accounts and use your own rates, at no extra cost.

What are Veeqo Credits?

Every time you buy a label, you’ll **earn up to 5% back** in Veeqo Credits. Use them to save even more on future shipping costs.

Can I use Veeqo if I don’t sell on Amazon?

Absolutely. Veeqo isn’t just for Amazon sellers—you can manage and ship orders from eBay, Etsy, Shopify, Walmart, WooCommerce, and more.

Your information stays secure and confidential, and your off-Amazon product and sales data is never shared with any other Amazon business unit.

Is support available?

Yes—access support when you need it, free of charge. Vera, our AI support agent, is available 24/7. Our team of human experts are available 17 hours on weekdays and 9.5 hours on weekends. You’ll also find step-by-step guides in our online [Help Center](https://help.veeqo.com/en/).

How can I pay for my shipping labels?

You can pay with a credit card or use Veeqo Credits you’ve earned. Amazon sellers can also choose to pay directly from their Seller Central balance.

No card details are needed to sign up. You’ll only add a payment method when you’re ready to buy shipping labels.

Is Veeqo an Amazon company?

Yes. Veeqo was founded by a seller in 2013 and acquired by Amazon in 2021. Today, we’re Amazon’s free multichannel shipping software—built for sellers, backed by Amazon.

Because Veeqo is an Amazon company, we can offer Amazon sellers exclusive benefits. When you ship Amazon orders through Veeqo, your account health metrics are automatically protected if delivery issues occur that aren’t your fault.

How long does it take to get set up?

You can connect your stores in minutes, no technical skills needed. Most sellers start shipping the same day they sign up.
