## Order Manager expandcollapse

### [**All sales channels in one place**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#aFCcollapseOne)

Whether you handle orders from a marketplace, an online store, placed by phone or in person, you can download them all to one user-friendly panel. Managing online sales has never been so easy!

### [**Fulfill orders faster than ever before**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#aFCcollapseTwo)

Base.com can handle numerous tasks for you: it will automatically send messages to customers, change order statuses, issue invoices, create shipments and print necessary documents!

### [**Impress the customer**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#aFCcollapseThree)

With the support of the Pick&Pack Assistant, you can not only pack your parcel correctly, but also shoot its photo! Base Caller will show you the customer's orders on your phone screen when the customer calls you.

[Learn more](https://base.com/en-US/functions/orders-manager/)

## Product Manager expandcollapse

### [**A convenient product database**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncMgcollapseOne)

If you do not own a store, you can manage your product selection directly from Base.com. A virtual warehouse is at your service, offering you the possibility of managing product data. If you have a store, you can catalogue items from outside the store offer.

### [**Fast product import**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncMgcollapseTwo)

Add products into Base.com's warehouse in whatever way that suits you: manually, with a file or by importing offers with the option of creating products in the warehouse enabled.

### [**List products on the marketplace right from the warehouse**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncMgcollapseThree)

The Base.com warehouse is a product source visible immediately on the listing form. The system automatically assigns shipping templates, offer templates and categories as well as auto-fills parameters. List hundreds of offers with a few clicks!

[Learn more](https://base.com/en-US/functions/product-manager/)

## Marketplace Manager expandcollapse

### [**Rapid listing from any warehouse**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncMrpcollapseOne)

Connect a store, wholesaler, ERP system or use the Base.com warehouse. Take a moment to set up the listing rules to accelerate the process.

### [**Manage online sales from one panel**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncMrpcollapseTwo)

Whichever marketplace (e.g. Amazon or eBay) you sell on, you can control all offers from one system. Check the offer statuses and edit them in bulk.

### [**No more stock and price discrepancies!**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncMrpcollapseThree)

Base.com ensures automatic stock and price synchronization on multiple marketplace platforms at the same time. It can also close offers for you if the stock or price reaches zero.

[Learn more](https://base.com/en-US/functions/marketplace-manager/)

## Shipping management expandcollapse

### [**The shipping process focused in one place**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncSendcollapseOne)

We have gathered multiple delivery operators in the system so that you can send shipments from one panel instead of logging in to each carrier's account. You can create shipments one by one, in bulk or set the system to create them automatically!

### [**Keep the customer in touch with the shipment**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncSendcollapseTwo)

No longer need to remember about sending notifications - the system will send an automatic message whenever a package status changes. The customer can also check out the status on the order information page.

### [**Enjoy the automation**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncSendcollapseThree)

Base.com will assist you in forwarding the shipping number to the marketplace and store. In addition, it will print out the shipping labels, e.g. when you mark the order as 'packed'.

[Learn more](https://base.com/en-US/functions/shipping-management/)

## Workflow automation expandcollapse

### [**Automatic status changes**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accFAcollapseOne)

Create custom statuses to help you organize your order list. The automatic action module will move orders between them when the specified event occurs.

### [**Reduce the order processing time!**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accFAcollapseTwo)

Use the Pick&Pack Assistant and automatic actions, to significantly decrease the processing time for each package. The system applies a barcode scanner to minimize the manual workload.

### [**Let yourself free from repetitive activities**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accFAcollapseThree)

Instead of sending messages to customers, issuing invoices, creating shipments or printing labels, focus on expanding your business and your product offer, while the system will perform recurring tasks for you.

[Learn more](https://base.com/en-US/functions/workflow-automation/)

## Base Connect expandcollapse

### [**B2B partnerships made easy and fast**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncConnectcollapseOne)

Connect with new customers, suppliers, manufacturers, dropshipping wholesalers or fulfillment service providers who also use Base.com.

### [**Improve order processing performance and customer satisfaction**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncConnectcollapseTwo)

Accelerate data exchange with contractors - Base Connect allows you to automatically transfer information about orders, products or shipments to your partners, reducing order processing time.

### [**Place wholesale orders in minutes**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncConnectcollapseThree)

Select and order products from the catalog visible in Base Connect, reducing the risk of errors. Track progress of your order in real time.

[Learn more](https://base.com/en-US/functions/base-connect/)

## Price automation (Repricing) expandcollapse

### [**Quickly adjust prices to the market situation**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncRepricingcollapseOne)

If prices from other vendors go up or down, the repricing function will automatically adjust your prices for each situation - on your terms.

### [**Save time and resources**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncRepricingcollapseTwo)

Free up your and your team's time. Set rules to change prices and forget about manually updating each listing.

### [**Take care of your pricing and margins**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncRepricingcollapseThree)

When others' offers become more expensive or run out of stock, the system will automatically raise the prices of your products and protect your margin.

[Learn more](https://base.com/en-US/functions/price-automation/)

## AI for e-commerce expandcollapse

### [**Speed up your work many times over**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncAIcollapseOne)

Base.com's AI functions make it easy to modify multiple products or offers at the same time. Instead of making changes one at a time, you can perform them in several hundred places at once and save even weeks of work.

### [**Expand cross-border sales**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncAIcollapseTwo)

With Base.com's artificial intelligence, you can easily develop business in new markets. Quickly prepare descriptions in multiple languages, list offers on local platforms and provide service to your customers.

### [**Automatically list on marketplaces**](https://base.com/en-US/home/\#accordionFuncAIcollapseThree)

We have developed an innovative AI-based auto-listing module that works for you. Set the rules for the module to follow, and the process will run automatically as soon as new products come into inventory.
