## MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Master Service Agreement ("**Agreement**") is entered into between **NPX Solutions Limited**, a company registered in England and Wales (registered number [XXXXXXXX]) with registered office at [ADDRESS] ("**Palvento**"), and the customer identified on the applicable Order Form ("**Customer**"), effective as of the date of Customer's first accepted Order Form (the "**Effective Date**").

### 1. Definitions

- "**Services**" — the Palvento software-as-a-service product and any ancillary services identified on an Order Form.
- "**Order Form**" — the ordering document specifying the Services purchased, subscription term, fees, and any customer-specific terms.
- "**Customer Data**" — all data and content submitted by Customer or Customer's users to the Services.
- "**Documentation**" — the user-facing product documentation at `palvento.com/docs` (or successor URL).
- "**DPA**" — the Data Processing Agreement incorporated by reference at `palvento.com/legal/dpa` (or provided on request).

### 2. Services

2.1 **Provision.** Subject to Customer's payment of fees and compliance with this Agreement, Palvento grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable, worldwide right to access and use the Services during the subscription term for Customer's internal business purposes.

2.2 **Usage quotas.** The Services may include plan-based quotas (channel count, listing count, AI enrichment credits, seats). Quotas are described in the Documentation and the Order Form. Palvento may, on 30 days' prior notice, adjust published quotas for all customers; for existing customers mid-term, quotas may only be reduced at the next renewal.

2.3 **Updates.** Palvento may update the Services at any time provided the updates do not materially reduce core functionality for Customer during the subscription term.

### 3. Customer obligations

3.1 **Compliance with law.** Customer will use the Services only in accordance with applicable law, the Documentation, and this Agreement.

3.2 **Authorised users.** Customer is responsible for its users' acts and omissions and for securing user credentials.

3.3 **Prohibited use.** Customer will not: (a) reverse-engineer, decompile, or derive source code from the Services; (b) resell or sublicense the Services other than via the founding-partner / partner terms on an Order Form; (c) use the Services to process protected health information, card-holder data, or other data requiring compliance regimes that Palvento has not expressly agreed to support; (d) interfere with other customers' use of the Services.

### 4. Fees & payment

4.1 **Fees.** Customer will pay the fees set out in the applicable Order Form. Fees are non-refundable except as expressly provided.

4.2 **Invoicing.** Palvento will invoice according to the billing cadence specified in the Order Form (monthly or annually). Invoices are due net 30 days from invoice date unless otherwise stated.

4.3 **Late payment.** Undisputed amounts unpaid for 30 days may accrue interest at 1.5% per month (or the maximum permitted by law, whichever is lower).

4.4 **Taxes.** Fees are exclusive of sales, VAT, GST, and similar taxes. Customer is responsible for applicable taxes other than those on Palvento's net income.

4.5 **Price changes.** Palvento may change published prices with 60 days' notice for renewals. Mid-term pricing is locked to the Order Form.

### 5. Term & termination

5.1 **Term.** The initial subscription term is set out in the Order Form. At the end of each term, the subscription will automatically renew for successive periods of the same length unless either party gives 30 days' prior written notice of non-renewal (or the period specified in the Order Form, if different).

5.2 **Termination for cause.** Either party may terminate for a material breach that is uncured 30 days after written notice.

5.3 **Effect of termination.** On termination: (a) Customer's access ceases; (b) Palvento will make Customer Data available for export for 30 days via the DSAR export endpoint; (c) after 30 days, Palvento may delete Customer Data in accordance with its data-retention policy; (d) accrued fees remain payable.

5.4 **Survival.** Sections 6 (Confidentiality), 7 (IP), 8 (Warranties), 9 (Indemnities), 10 (Limitation of Liability), and 11 (General) survive termination.

### 6. Confidentiality

Each party will protect the other party's Confidential Information with the same degree of care it uses for its own (but not less than reasonable care) and use it only to perform under this Agreement. Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that is public, independently developed, rightfully received from a third party, or required to be disclosed by law (with prompt notice where permitted).

### 7. Intellectual property

7.1 **Palvento IP.** Palvento retains all right, title, and interest in the Services, Documentation, and any feedback or suggestions Customer provides.

7.2 **Customer Data.** Customer retains all right, title, and interest in Customer Data. Customer grants Palvento a non-exclusive, worldwide licence to process Customer Data solely to provide the Services.

7.3 **Aggregate data.** Palvento may use de-identified, aggregated data derived from Customer's use of the Services to improve the product, train models, and publish benchmarks, provided no such use identifies Customer.

### 8. Warranties

8.1 **Mutual.** Each party warrants it has authority to enter into this Agreement.

8.2 **Palvento.** Palvento warrants the Services will perform materially in accordance with the Documentation. Customer's sole remedy for breach of this warranty is termination of the affected Order Form and a pro-rata refund of prepaid, unused fees.

8.3 **Disclaimer.** EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY SET OUT, THE SERVICES ARE PROVIDED "AS IS" WITHOUT WARRANTY OF ANY KIND, WHETHER EXPRESS, IMPLIED, OR STATUTORY.

### 9. Indemnities

9.1 **By Palvento.** Palvento will defend Customer against any third-party claim alleging the Services, as provided by Palvento, infringe a patent, copyright, or trademark, and pay damages or costs finally awarded, provided Customer notifies Palvento promptly, gives Palvento sole control of the defence, and cooperates reasonably.

9.2 **By Customer.** Customer will defend Palvento against any third-party claim arising from Customer Data or Customer's use of the Services in breach of Section 3.

### 10. Limitation of liability

10.1 **Cap.** Each party's aggregate liability under this Agreement is capped at the fees paid or payable by Customer in the 12 months preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

10.2 **Excluded damages.** Neither party is liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages, or for lost profits or revenue.

10.3 **Exceptions.** The cap and exclusions do not apply to: (a) Customer's payment obligations; (b) either party's indemnity obligations; (c) breach of confidentiality; (d) gross negligence or wilful misconduct; (e) death or personal injury caused by negligence where limitation is prohibited.

### 11. General

11.1 **Data processing.** The DPA forms part of this Agreement.

11.2 **Governing law.** This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales. The courts of England and Wales have exclusive jurisdiction, except either party may seek injunctive relief in any competent court.

11.3 **Assignment.** Neither party may assign this Agreement without the other's consent, not unreasonably withheld, except that either party may assign to an affiliate or successor in a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all assets.

11.4 **Notices.** Notices to Palvento: `legal@palvento.com`. Notices to Customer: the email on the Order Form.

11.5 **Entire agreement.** This Agreement plus any Order Form and the DPA is the entire agreement. Any conflict is resolved in this order: Order Form > DPA > this Agreement.

11.6 **Modifications.** Modifications must be in writing signed by both parties.

11.7 **Force majeure.** Neither party is liable for delay or failure caused by events beyond its reasonable control.

11.8 **Severability.** If any provision is held unenforceable, the remainder continues in effect.

11.9 **No waiver.** Failure to enforce a provision is not a waiver.

11.10 **Relationship.** The parties are independent contractors. Nothing in this Agreement creates a partnership or agency.
