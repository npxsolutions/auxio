# Acquisition

Paid and earned channels that bring net-new visitors to Palvento. Separate from distribution (which leans on partners/directories).

## Files

- `seo-strategy.md` — topic clusters, pillar pages, keyword targets, internal linking plan, `vs/*` page expansion. The 4 blog posts are a start, not a strategy.
- `content-calendar.md` — rolling 12-week publishing schedule. Title, target keyword, assignee, status, publish date, performance.
- `cold-outreach-playbook.md` — ICP lists, sequences, tooling (Instantly/Smartlead), reply handling, compliance (CAN-SPAM/UK PECR).

## Key questions

- SEO: what are the 5 keywords a 7-figure Shopify seller types when they're fed up with Linnworks?
- Content: what post gets shared inside a 3PL Slack channel?
- Cold outreach: who in the org do we contact — ops lead or founder? What's our open-rate bar before we scale?
