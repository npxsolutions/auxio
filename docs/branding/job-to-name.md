# Job-grounded names — pick a metaphor first, then a name

Better approach than a sound-pool: decide what your software actually *is* to the operator, then pick a name that names that thing.

## What the software does (plain English, no marketing)

1. **Pulls** product data out of Shopify
2. **Validates** every listing against each marketplace's rules (eBay / Amazon / TikTok / etc.)
3. **Translates** one Shopify catalog into 9 marketplace-shaped catalogs
4. **Pushes** clean data to the marketplaces
5. **Monitors** for rejections, drift, silent suppressions
6. **Reconciles** orders coming back from every channel into one ledger
7. **Fixes** the most common errors automatically

If you had to describe the whole product in one verb, the three strongest candidates are: **Validate** · **Translate** · **Guard**.

## My read: the strongest metaphors for your wedge

For **"self-serve multichannel feed management for Shopify-led sellers"**, these three metaphors are the most ownable because no direct competitor is using them:

### 🥇 Customs-house / clearance
Fits perfectly. Your product is a clearance gate between a Shopify catalog and the marketplaces it ships into. Every operator says "my listing got rejected" — that's customs vocabulary. Best shortlist:

- **Lading** — 2 syllables, distinctive, commerce-native, I don't know of a single SaaS using it
- **Manifest** — strong meaning; watch TM (Manifest AI, Manifest crypto wallet)
- **Docket** — short, unclaimed, "the order of business"
- **Clearing** — exact job description

### 🥈 Gatekeeper / validator
You're the validator. Your product exists because the marketplace will reject bad listings and you stop that from happening. Best shortlist:

- **Warden** — watchful guardian
- **Sentry** — active vigilance, memorable
- **Vellum** — parchment/proof, elegant and rare

### 🥉 Weaver
Nine threads into one fabric. Strong metaphor if your visual system leans into it.

- **Skein** — one coil of threads, obscure and distinctive
- **Braid** — three strands, short, unclaimed in SaaS
- **Ply** — a layer, minimalist
