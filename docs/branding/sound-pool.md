## Vibe guardrails (so your ear isn't lying to you)

Before you mark a favorite, ask:

1. **Say it on a phone call**: "Hi, this is [name], calling from [name]." Does it sound confident or awkward?
2. **Type it 3 times**: Do you ever mistype it? If yes, customers will too.
3. **Say it in three languages** if your inner voice does that — any wince moments?
4. **Imagine the domain**: "go to [name].com". Does it sound like a real destination?
5. **Imagine the logo**: does your brain see something, or go blank?
6. **Would you say it at a dinner party** if someone asked what you do? Or would you caveat it?

A name passes all 6, you move on to live verification. A name fails 2 or more, cross it out without looking back.
