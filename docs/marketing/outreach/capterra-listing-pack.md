## Long description (4 blocks — paste verbatim)

### Who Palvento is for

```
Shopify operators doing $100k–$500k/mo GMV, running three to five marketplaces alongside their Shopify store. You've outgrown the free first-party connector's channel ceiling. The enterprise feed engines quoted you $2,500+/mo with a thirty-day onboarding — and you bounced. Your catalogue is probably in a Google Sheet right now with one tab per marketplace, always a week out of date. Palvento is the middle option the category has never built.
```

### What it does

```
Shopify installs in one OAuth click direct from palvento.com. Pick the marketplaces you're live on — eBay, Google Shopping, Amazon, TikTok Shop, Etsy, Walmart, OnBuy, BigCommerce, WooCommerce. Palvento pulls your Shopify catalogue, maps categories per marketplace, and catches feed errors at ingest: missing or malformed GTINs, oversize or wrong-ratio images, banned-word violations, category-attribute gaps, title-length overruns.

Clean listings get pushed to every channel. Inventory syncs sub-minute so nothing oversells across channels. Every order comes back into one settled P&L view with line-item fee attribution — FBA storage, eBay insertion, TikTok commission, shipping, returns, FX spread — so you actually know which marketplace is profitable after the cost of serving it, not just which one has the most orders.
```

### Why it's different

```
Self-serve signup from palvento.com. No sales call. No solutions architect. No managed-services retainer. Published pricing in five currencies (USD, GBP, EUR, AUD, CAD). Flat tiers indexed to order volume — not a percentage of your revenue. Feed rules, error hub, and per-channel P&L in one product, not three tools duct-taped together.

Error hub surfaces what's wrong, why, and the exact fix — at ingest, before the listing gets suppressed. 14-day free trial, no credit card required.
```

### Who it's not for

```
Pure DTC Shopify stores under $25k/mo — the free first-party connector is enough for your shape. Enterprise retailers needing SSO, SAML, data-residency compliance, or EDI — we serve that tier on the Enterprise plan, but the core wedge is mid-market self-serve. Pure B2B wholesale with no marketplace exposure — the feed loop Palvento closes doesn't apply.
```
