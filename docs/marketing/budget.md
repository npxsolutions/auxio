# Marketing Budget

Three tiers — $0 baseline, $10k founder-mode, $50k post-seed, $250k Series-A — with a channel-by-channel allocation, expected outcome, and a kill criterion for each line. Numbers are quarterly unless noted.

Kill criterion = "if this is true at week X, reallocate the remaining spend."

---

## Tier 0 — $0 / quarter (the compounding baseline)

The assumption: no cash. What still moves the needle.

| Line | Allocation | Expected outcome | Kill criterion |
|---|---|---|---|
| Founder-written content (2/wk) | 0 | 24 posts live by Day 90, 3k monthly organic by Week 12 | Week 8 organic < 800/mo |
| Community presence (3 comments/week × 3 communities) | 0 | 15 inbound trial signups attributed | Week 6: zero inbound mentions |
| Cold outreach (founder, 100 prospects/wk) | 0 | 6–10 demos, 3 closed-won | Week 4: < 2% reply rate |
| Partnerships (founder-led, 2 convos/wk) | 0 | 5 signed partners by Day 90 | Week 6: < 3 agreements |
| PH + HN launch | 0 | 500 PH upvotes, 80 HN points | Launch day: < 100 PH upvotes |
| Founder LinkedIn (1 post/wk) | 0 | 3 trials attributed, 1 demo | Week 6: < 2k impressions avg |
| Twitter/X threads (2/wk) | 0 | Audience growth 500 → 2k | Week 8: < 200 new followers |
| Affiliate program | 0 (rev-share 20%) | 3 paying affiliates by Day 60 | Day 60: < 1 affiliate sale |

**What compounds at $0.** Content (long-term search), community reputation, direct outbound skill, partner relationships. Everything else needs money.

## Rules across all tiers

1. **No line item goes over 40% of tier spend.** Single-channel dependency is a kill-shot.
2. **Every paid line has a kill criterion and a weekly review.** If the kill fires twice, the money moves.
3. **Founder time is accounted for at $100/hr internally.** If a channel "feels free", it isn't.
4. **Revenue % pricing is banned internally, not just externally.** No affiliate, no partner, no deal structured on % of customer GMV. Same principle we sell on.
5. **Brand does not get a line item until $250k tier.** Too early to separate "brand" from "founder presence".
