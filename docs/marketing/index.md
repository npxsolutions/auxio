## Ownership model

For the first 90 days, the founder owns every primary deliverable. A part-time content lead handles supporting posts, production, and scheduling. Engineering owns any in-product growth surface. After Day 90, a dedicated growth engineer picks up the loops. Marketing headcount delayed until after the Series A.
