# 90-Day Post-Launch Plan

Week-by-week sprint plan. Owners are abbreviated: **F** = founder, **C** = content lead, **D** = designer, **Eng** = engineering. If a week looks overloaded, the founder has said "yes" too often — see the stop-doing list at the bottom.

KPIs reference `kpis.md`.

---

## Week 1 — Launch week
**Theme.** Ship the launch. Everything else waits.
**Deliverables.**
- Product Hunt launch (Tuesday 12:01am PT) — kit in `launch/product-hunt-kit.md`.
- Hacker News "Show HN" post (Tuesday 6am PT, 6 hours after PH) — copy in `launch/hn-post.md`.
- Founder LinkedIn launch post (see `outreach/linkedin-launch-sequence.md` post 1).
- Email blast to waitlist (subject: "Meridia is live") — send 7am PT Tuesday.
- Three pre-written community pings (Ecommerce Fuel, Indie Hackers, Operators Slack) — drop by 10am PT Tuesday.
- Two founder tweets/day for five days (bank in `outreach/twitter-thread-bank.md`).
**KPIs.** 500+ PH upvotes (stretch: top 5 product of the day). 80+ HN points. 250+ waitlist → trial conversions. 1,500 net new site visitors.
**Owner.** F drives everything. C handles comment moderation, D fixes any live-site bugs.

## Stop-doing list — first 90 days

We are actively *not* doing these. If asked, the answer is "not this quarter":

- **Paid Meta ads.** Intent mismatch. Revisit once Tier 1 self-serve funnel proves out.
- **LinkedIn ads.** Retargeting only once warm audience exists. No cold LinkedIn ads.
- **Sponsoring a big conference (NRF, Shoptalk booth).** Too expensive, wrong ICP density. Q3 earliest.
- **Hiring an SDR team.** Founder-led outbound until the process is repeatable and the script converts.
- **Building a public reseller/whitelabel program.** Distracts from the core partner motion.
- **Podcast launch.** Seductive, 6-month payback minimum, steals 8 hours/week. Revisit at 200 paying customers.
- **Paid review sites (Capterra, G2, GetApp) at the "featured listing" tier.** Free listings only.
- **Publishing AI-generated content.** Non-negotiable. Every post has the founder's fingerprints.
- **Building a mobile app.** There is no Tier 2 operator demanding a mobile app. Don't invent demand.
- **Rebranding mid-quarter.** If the rename happens, it happens as a coordinated event — not piecemeal.
