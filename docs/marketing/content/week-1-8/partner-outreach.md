## General outreach rules

1. **No attachments on first email.** PDFs get caught in spam and signal "sales automation."
2. **Three touches maximum.** If three emails don't get a reply, stop. Revisit in 90 days with a new hook (feature launch, customer story, shared customer reference).
3. **Personalise the first line.** Reference a specific client, blog post, case study, or conference talk from the recipient. If you can't find one, don't send.
4. **Founder signs every email.** No "sent on behalf of." The founder is the brand for the first 20 partners.
5. **Track in CRM.** Every outreach goes in the CRM (Attio) with stage, reply, and outcome. Review weekly.
