```

```

*27/30. Leads with brand, then the exact query. Not "Palvento — Sell on Amazon, eBay & More" (generic, undifferentiated). Not "Multichannel Sync" (keyword stuff without brand equity).*

## What I'm not doing in the listing

- No customer logos (we have zero paying customers; fabricating logos would show up on diligence later)
- No aggregate statistics we can't defend ("trusted by X thousand sellers")
- No animated hero graphics (static screenshots outperform video thumbnails in App Store A/B tests by ~30% per Shopify Partner case studies)
- No "AI-powered" claim as the lead — we lead with the outcome (clean feeds, live fast), AI appears as a feature
- No competitive copy naming rivals — Shopify's app review team rejects listings that disparage other Shopify apps by name. And the founder rule holds.
