## Description

Managing product data across marketplaces is broken. You export a CSV from Shopify, tweak columns for Amazon, fix titles for eBay, adjust attributes for Etsy — and repeat every time something changes. One wrong field and your listing gets suppressed. The tools that solve this cost $2,500/mo and take weeks to implement.

Palvento replaces that entire workflow. Connect your Shopify store, map your catalog to any marketplace, and let Palvento keep every listing in sync — validated, enriched, and optimized per channel.

### Key features

**Multi-channel feed sync**
Push your Shopify catalog to Amazon, eBay, Etsy, TikTok Shop, and Walmart from a single dashboard. Changes propagate automatically. No more CSV exports.

**50+ feed validation rules per channel**
Every marketplace has different requirements — title length, required attributes, image specs, category taxonomy. Palvento validates every listing against channel-specific rules before it goes live, so you catch errors before they cost you impressions.

**AI-powered data enrichment**
Generate optimized titles, descriptions, and attributes tailored to each marketplace's algorithm and buyer expectations. Not generic AI copy — enrichment trained on what actually converts per channel.

**AI image analysis and compliance**
Automatically check product images against marketplace requirements — background color, resolution, watermarks, text overlays. Flag non-compliant images before they trigger listing suppression.

**Per-channel P&L and margin tracking**
See true profit per SKU per channel. Palvento pulls in marketplace fees, shipping costs, returns, and advertising spend to show you the margin you actually keep — not the margin you think you have.

**Feed health dashboard**
A single score that tells you how healthy your listings are across every channel. Benchmarked against category averages so you know where you stand relative to competitors.
